Hi, I'm Hadiya

I'm a software developer, a problem-solver, and a lifelong learner. I'm interested in the future of mixed reality, architecting useful software solutions to real problems, and trying to design good user experiences. I like wet-folding origami, WWII fiction, comparative linguistics, plants, and poststructuralism.

This portfolio is a timeline of my work and my curiosities. If you want to know more about my work or just talk, check out my LinkedIn or shoot me an email.
